Centre Description

Set over 500 acres in the University of Hertfordshire, our Hatfield campus is located just outside the historic town of Hatfield and is the perfect choice for students who want to experience learning English in a university campus in a safe environment and for those wishing to explore London, which is only a 90-minute drive away.

Hatfield is a small town with a big place in history, with Royalty, nobility, political leaders, musicians and authors contributing to Hatfield's amazing past. Dating back to before the Norman Conquest in 1066, Hatfield played an important part in Tudor and Victorian history.
