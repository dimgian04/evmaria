Educational Partner Testimonials

Authentic feedback from our trusted educational partners and language schools

"We would like to express our heartfelt gratitude for the exceptional experience our students and we enjoyed at the Summer Camp at the University of Westminster, Harrow Campus from July 3 to July 10. The accommodation was comfortable and well-organized, providing a perfect environment for our students to relax and bond. The excursions were thoughtfully planned, offering cultural enrichment and memorable experiences. The lessons were engaging, informative, and excellently delivered. Overall, this week was a fantastic blend of education, adventure, and personal growth for our students. Thank you!"


Marigianna Karatza & Petros Syntelis

Owners of the Scholars Group Kanakari

"We are thrilled to share our amazing experience at the University of Kingston's Summer Camp from July 3 to July 10! The accommodation exceeded our expectations - it was comfortable, welcoming, and provided the perfect environment for our students. The excursions were both fun and educational, offering a fantastic opportunity for students to explore new places and immerse themselves in British culture. The lessons were fantastic, thanks to a team of highly professional and engaging teachers, making learning enjoyable and enriching. This experience has made a lasting, positive impression on our students and staff, and we highly recommend it!"

Areti Korma

Owner, ELB Language School
