Centre Description

The University of Edinburgh's Pollock Halls is a vibrant and bustling summer campus located in the heart of Scotland's historic capital city. Set within beautifully landscaped gardens, the campus offers a range of modern, comfortable accommodations and facilities, making it an ideal location for summer programs, conferences, and events. Pollock Halls is comprised of several distinct buildings, each offering its own unique charm and character. One of the most notable buildings on the campus is Pollack Hall, which is a traditional sandstone building dating back to the early 1900s.

This building has been modernized to provide contemporary facilities while retaining its original charm and elegance. The accommodation at Pollack Halls offers single or twin rooms with shared bathroom facilities, all designed with comfort and convenience in mind. Rooms are well-appointed with comfortable beds, ample storage space, a television, and access to high-speed Wi-Fi, ensuring that guests have everything they need to make their stay enjoyable and productive.

In addition to its comfortable accommodations, Pollock Halls offers a range of amenities and services to meet the needs of its guests. The campus features several dining options, including a cafeteria-style restaurant, a cafe, and a bar, all serving delicious and nutritious meals throughout the day. Classes are held on campus in modern classrooms that are easily accessible on foot.

For those looking to stay active and engaged, Pollock Halls has a variety of recreational facilities, including a fitness center, tennis courts, and a sports hall, as well as outdoor spaces for walking, jogging, or simply relaxing in the sun. Overall, the University of Edinburgh's Pollock Halls is a vibrant and welcoming summer campus that provides a comfortable and convenient base for exploring all that this historic city has to offer.

Campus Highlights/Facilities

  • Centrally located
  • On-site amenities such as coffee shops, supermarkets

Age Range

The center is advisable for young learners aged 12 to 17.

Residential Option

On-campus accommodation is in single or twin rooms with ensuite bathroom facilities.

English Courses

  • Online level testing before students' arrival
  • 16 English lessons per week (45 mins/lesson)
  • Maximum class size: 20 students
  • PLUS e-book and all supplementary materials provided
  • PLUS End-of-Course certificate
