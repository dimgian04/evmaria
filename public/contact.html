Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What age groups do you accept?

We accept students aged 12-18 for our summer language camps. Programs are tailored to different age groups to ensure appropriate learning experiences.

What language levels do you accommodate?

We welcome students of all English levels, from beginner to advanced. Students take a placement test upon arrival to ensure they're placed in the appropriate class.

What's included in the program fee?

Our program fees include accommodation, meals, language classes, cultural activities, excursions, airport transfers, and 24/7 supervision. Airfare is not included.

How do you ensure student safety?

Student safety is our top priority. We provide 24/7 supervision, maintain strict safety protocols, and all our staff are thoroughly vetted and trained in child protection.

Can parents visit during the program?

While we don't encourage visits during the program as it can disrupt the immersive experience, we can arrange visits with advance notice and proper coordination.

What happens if a student gets sick?

We have comprehensive medical support and insurance coverage. Our staff are trained in first aid, and we have access to local medical facilities at all our locations.
