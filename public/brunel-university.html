Summer Language Camp at Uxbridge Brunel

Modern university with excellent facilities and diverse student community in Uxbridge.

Centre Description

Brunel University London, named after the Victorian engineer Isambard Kingdom Brunel, is located in Uxbridge, West London and is a campus-based university. The halls of residence, refectory, classrooms and sports facilities are all within walking distance to each other. The campus has been recently refurbished and offers modern facilities. It is an ideal location for young learners who want to be close to central London with direct access but at the same time living in a very safe environment.

Campus Highlights/Facilities

  • Close to Central London
  • Modern, well-equipped accommodation
  • Green open spaces
  • All facilities within walking distance
  • Excellent sport facilities
  • Shops on campus
  • Free WiFi
  • Safe and secure environment
  • Easy Access to Heathrow Airport

Age Range

The center is advisable for young learners aged 10 to 17.

Residential Option

On-campus accommodation is in single rooms with shared or en-suite bathroom facilities.

English Courses

  • Online level testing before students' arrival
  • 16 English lessons per week (45 mins/lesson)
  • Maximum class size: 20 students
  • PLUS e-book and all supplementary materials provided
  • PLUS End-of-Course certificate
