Bath University

Southwest UK

Summer Language Camp at Bath University

Study in the beautiful historic city of Bath with its Roman heritage.

Centre Description

Bath, one of England's most beautiful cities, is in South West England, about an hour from London. Designated by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site, Bath has a variety of theatres, museums and other cultural venues including the world-famous Roman Baths. Numerous caf√©s, bistros, sophisticated shops and boutiques make Bath one of the most interesting and elegant sights in Europe.

The PLUS centre is located at the leading school for girls in the area. It is just outside the city centre and it can be easily reached by bus which stops right in front of the school. The Campus is self-contained and all facilities are within easy walking distance. This peaceful centre is particularly suited to younger students who benefit from the safety and the location of the closed campus.

Campus Highlights/Facilities

  • Boarding House with modern, comfortable accommodation
  • Playing fields, sports hall and tennis courts
  • Comfortable student lounge with TV and DVD

Age Range

The center is advisable for young learners aged 10 to 17.

Residential Option

On-campus accommodation is in single or twin bedrooms, with the addition of fewer three-, five- or six-bedded rooms. Shared showers/toilets/washbasins with a typical ratio of 1:5.

English Courses

  • Online level testing before students' arrival
  • 16 English lessons per week (45 mins/lesson)
  • Maximum class size: 20 students
  • PLUS e-book and all supplementary materials provided
  • PLUS End-of-Course certificate
