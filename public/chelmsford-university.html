Summer Language Camp at Chelmsford University

Modern university in a vibrant town with excellent transport links.

Photo Gallery

Centre Description

Chelmsford is a city in Essex in the east of England. It is approximately 50km north east of London and there is a railway station with direct trains to London Liverpool Street in Central London (35 minutes travel time). Chelmsford is an urban area with a mix of Victorian and modern buildings and a vibrant city centre with two shopping centres and a wide range of restaurants and pubs. Chelmsford is surrounded by many parks and picturesque English villages. Places to visit include Marsh Farm Animal Adventure Park, Tropical Wings Zoo, Chelmsford Cathedral, Shire Hall, Chelmsford Museum and many more.

The PLUS centre is located at one of the largest agricultural universities in the UK. It is surrounded by its own farm, landscaped gardens and offers a secure university environment with plenty of open areas for recreation. The campus is a self contained with lots of modern teaching, living and sporting facilities. The city centre is easily accessible from the campus by bus (15 minutes travel time).

Campus Highlights/Facilities

  • Experience the magnificent British countryside thanks to a picturesque 220 hectare campus, including beautiful landscaped gardens
  • Self-contained campus with all facilities within walking distance
  • Safe and secure environment
  • Academy centre
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi for the students and group leaders
  • Outdoor sports facilities for football, basketball, rugby, volleyball and tennis
  • Indoor sports hall
  • 24 hour CCTV.

Age Range

The center is advisable for young learners aged 11 to 17.

Residential Option

On-campus accommodation is in single rooms with shared or en-suite bathroom facilities.

English Courses

  • Online level testing before students' arrival
  • 16 English lessons per week (45 mins/lesson)
  • Maximum class size: 20 students
  • PLUS e-book and all supplementary materials provided
  • PLUS End-of-Course certificate
