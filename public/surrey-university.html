Centre Description

The University of Surrey is located in Guildford, a historic town in Surrey, England. Situated about 27 miles southwest of central London, it is well-connected to the capital. By train, it takes approximately 35 minutes to reach London Waterloo, making it convenient for day trips or commuting.

Guildford itself is a vibrant town with a mix of cultural and natural attractions. The picturesque River Wey runs through the town, offering scenic walking paths and boat trips. Guildford Castle, with its beautiful gardens and panoramic views, is a popular spot for visitors.

Nearby, the Surrey Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty provides stunning landscapes ideal for hiking and cycling. The town also has a lively shopping scene, with a range of high street and boutique stores.

Further afield, the historic towns of Farnham and Dorking are accessible, offering charming streets and additional sightseeing opportunities.
