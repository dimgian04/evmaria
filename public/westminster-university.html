Summer Language Camp at University of Westminster

Experience the vibrant London life at the University of Westminster's Harrow campus

Photo Gallery

Centre Description

The London Harrow campus is located at the University of Westminster’s Harrow campus. Situated conveniently beside Northwick Park tube station, the London Harrow campus offers easy access to the bustling city centre and all its renowned attractions.

Harrow is a large but tranquil town just in Greater London, with a rich blend of historic charm and modern amenities, iconic hilltop views, quaint streets, and easy access to central London.

The campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities and technology, plenty of green spaces and parks, and purpose-built residences providing comfortable accommodation right on campus. Enjoy delicious meals at the central dining room in the Forum, fostering a sense of community among students from around the globe.

The modern, well-equipped classrooms provide the perfect environment for learning, while the expansive sports hall and playing fields offer ample opportunities for recreation and exercise. With a student disco, social spaces, and a variety of facilities at your fingertips, the University of Westminster promises an exceptional summer experience.

Campus Highlights/Facilities

  • Single or twin en-suite bedrooms
  • Disabled access and facilities
  • Free Wi-Fi access
  • 24-hour on-site security
  • Spacious classrooms with interactive whiteboards
  • On-campus shops and cafes for convenience
  • Secure environment adjacent to Northwick Park tube station

Age Range

The center is advisable for young learners aged 10 to 17.

Residential Option

On-campus accommodation is in single or twin rooms with en-suite bathroom facilities.

English Courses

  • Online level testing before students' arrival
  • 16 English lessons per week (45 mins/lesson)
  • Maximum class size: 20 students
  • PLUS e-book and all supplementary materials provided
  • PLUS End-of-Course certificate
